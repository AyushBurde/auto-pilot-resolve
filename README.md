Executive Summary
AutoCare is a cutting-edge Agentic AI system designed for a leading Indian automotive OEM to transform aftersales service from reactive to proactive. Our solution creates a secure, autonomous, closed-loop system that continuously monitors vehicle health, proactively schedules maintenance, and feeds actionable quality insights back to manufacturing.

Primary Goals:

Maximize Vehicle Uptime: Proactively predict and schedule repairs before failures occur.

Enhance Customer Experience (CX): Deliver autonomous, personalized service scheduling via a persuasive Voice Agent.

Drive Product Quality: Close the loop between maintenance (RCA/CAPA) and manufacturing design.
